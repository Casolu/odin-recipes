Pizza

Follow this recipe to make a pizza with your favourite toppings. It is really easy and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Sieved tomatos
  • Oregano
  • Cheese
  • Your favourite toppings

Steps

  1. Add the flour to a bowl
  2. Add the water, yeast, salt and sugar
  3. Mix it thoroughly and let it rest for one hour
  4. Spread the dough on an oven tray
  5. Spread the sieved tomatos on the dough, sprinkle some salt and oregano onto the tomatos
  6. Add the cheese on top
  7. Add your favourite toppings
  8. Now bake the pizza at 200Â°C for about 10 minutes
  9. At the end sprinkle some oregano on top and enjoy your home made Pizza :)
